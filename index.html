Canadian Mortgage Calculator

Compare up to five mortgage scenarios.

This calculator mirrors the FCAC workflow so you can compare rates, terms, payment frequencies, and prepayment plans side by side without getting lost in the jargon.

Mortgage amount = what you borrow Term = your current contract length Amortization = total payoff timeline Prepayment = extra principal you pay early

Quick guide

  1. Set the mortgage details for the first scenario.
  2. Add or duplicate scenarios to compare different rates, terms, or prepayments.
  3. Use the comparison board and schedules to see cost, balance, and payoff changes.

Notes

These calculations follow Canadian mortgage conventions with semi-annual compounding, FCAC-style payment frequencies, and the prepayment options shown on the public FCAC mortgage calculator. Lenders may still calculate some values differently.

The CSV export includes every scenario’s inputs, summary values, yearly schedule, and payment-by-payment schedule in one file.
